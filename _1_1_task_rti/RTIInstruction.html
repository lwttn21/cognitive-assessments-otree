Five-Choice Reaction Time

This task measures your reaction speed and movement speed.

  1. Press and HOLD the button labeled "HOME" at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Keep holding it and wait.
  3. As soon as one of the circles at the top turns YELLOW:
    • Release the mouse button immediately.
    • Move the cursor to the yellow circle and click it as fast as you can.
Important: If you release the button before the yellow light appears, it will count as an error!
